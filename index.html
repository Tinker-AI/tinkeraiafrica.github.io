TinkerAI Africa

We are a group of data scientists who work together on data-centered projects and products. Our interests revolve around data analysis, machine learning and deep learning, either for production or research. Although still fairly new, we just completed one fun project together and have other ongoing projects in the pipeline.

TinkerAI started with the vision of creating an atmosphere for data scientists to have their hands on real life projects, leaving them free of the impression that data-centered projects are rare, especially here in Africa.

We are few, but growing and we are happy that you are reading about us. If you'd like to see our first project, you can find that here. We will keep updating this page for the rest of our ongoing projects.

Are you a fan already? Kindly follow us on Twitter. You could also send us an email or visit our Github page to know what we are working on. We are open if you'd like to join our team, collaborate (currently non-profitable) or offer us a gig.
